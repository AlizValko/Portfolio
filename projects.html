Weather app

Freetime project

Since I never know what to wear I had the idea for an app that uses the local weather predictions to suggest outfit ideas for the user. The user can of course customise their style, gender expression and treshold for low and high temperatures.

School project

For my course 'Bespoke Design' I created a high-fidelity prototype of an experience that lets users "unwrap" digital presents. It is a bespoke experience designed according to the taste of only one person.
